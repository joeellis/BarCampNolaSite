BarCamp is New Orleans’ tech "unconference," an ad-hoc melting pot of minds organized on the fly by attendees, for attendees.

So What Is Barcamp Nola already?

Ok, ok calm down. BarCampNola is a local incarnation of the international network of user generated conferences called BarCamps. BarCamp is an ad-hoc gathering born from the desire for people to share and learn in an open environment. It is an intense event with discussions, demos, and interaction from attendees. It is an open event where each attendee is very much encouraged to participate.

BarCampNola brings talented people together in one location to exchange ideas, build connections, launch shared projects or initiatives, and generally assist in realizing the potential of the tech community here in New Orleans. It is both a showcase of our local talent as well as a mechanism to inspire others, and it's free to attend, so why aren't you going already?? Aren't you cool like us? Register up man!
